Visit us!

We are located

We are located at SuperStreet 0455 Oslo

Our opening hours are Monday-saturday from 09:00 - 17:00

Admission fee

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 up to 20 people, please book a tour down below. More than 20? please contact us to arrange the tour.

Book a guided tour

Food and Drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Museum shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
